Cookie Policy

Last updated: 17 May 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how mangabuddy1.com uses cookies and similar technologies, and how you can control them. It supplements our Privacy Policy.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, to remember your preferences, to measure performance, and to deliver advertising.

2. Categories of cookies we use

2.1 Strictly necessary

Required for the site to function (for example, to remember your cookie-consent choice). These cookies cannot be switched off in our systems.

2.2 Analytics

We may use Google Analytics 4 with IP anonymisation enabled to understand aggregate traffic patterns. Cookies set: _ga, _ga_*. Retention: up to 24 months.

2.3 Advertising

If advertising is displayed on this site, third-party providers (including Google AdSense) may set cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites, to measure ad performance, and to detect fraud. Cookies and identifiers used by Google may include IDE, NID, __gads, __gpi.

3. How to control cookies

  • Consent banner: when first visiting the site from the EEA, UK or other regions that require it, you can accept or reject non-essential cookies via the on-page banner.
  • Browser settings: most browsers let you delete existing cookies and block new ones. See the help pages for Chrome, Firefox, Safari and Edge.
  • Opt out of personalised advertising: Google Ads Settings, aboutads.info/choices (US), youronlinechoices.eu (EU).

4. Do Not Track

We currently do not respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals because there is no industry consensus on how such signals should be interpreted. We do honour the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal for users in jurisdictions where it carries legal effect.

5. Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision.
