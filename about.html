About mangabuddy1.com

Last updated: 17 May 2026

mangabuddy1.com is an independent, community-run status, mirror and FAQ tracker for the third-party manga reading site commonly known as MangaBuddy. We are not affiliated with, owned by, sponsored by or endorsed by the operators of mangabuddy.com or any official MangaBuddy entity.

Why this site exists

When MangaBuddy experiences a 502 outage, a redirect issue or a domain rotation, readers head to Reddit and Discord looking for answers. Those answers are scattered across dozens of threads. This site collects the verifiable facts in one place, updates them on a 15-minute uptime probe schedule, and points readers to safer, legal alternatives where appropriate.

Editorial standards

  • We label rumours as rumours. Unverified Discord screenshots are not treated as fact.
  • We disclose our outage-monitoring methodology (three regional probes, 15-minute interval).
  • We link to original sources (Cloudflare docs, Reddit threads, official publisher pages).
  • Corrections are applied within 24 hours of being notified through the contact page.

Team

Linh N. — editor. Reading manga since 2006, manhwa since the early Lezhin years. Helps moderate a 12,000-member scanlation Discord. Has maintained a personal MangaBuddy outage log since 2022.

How we make money

This page may display contextual advertising. We do not run affiliate links to unlicensed reading sites. Any external link to a paid platform (WEBTOON, Lezhin, Tappytoon, MANGA Plus) is provided for reader safety reasons, not commercial reward, and we do not currently participate in those programmes.
